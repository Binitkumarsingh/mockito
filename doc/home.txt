=Mockito=

  * intuitive way of testing: setup-execute-assert.
  * descriptive, refactorable, proven EasyMockish syntax.
  * assert-what-you-want pattern allows creating more specific test methods concentrated on testing only selected behavior/interactions.

I thought it's a shiny new idea but it's so hard to be inventive these days. Similar concept:

  * [http://mocklib.sourceforge.net MockLib]
  * [http://code.google.com/p/turtlemock turtlemock]

It's definitely too late to reinvent the wheel. Hats down before EasyMock folks for their ideas on beautiful and refactorable mocking syntax. Similar interface:

  * [http://easymock.org EasyMock]
